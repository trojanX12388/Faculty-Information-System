Institution Commitment to Interdisciplinary Collaboration

Institutional commitment to interdisciplinary collaboration signifies PUPQC's steadfast dedication to nurturing an academic environment where the merging of knowledge, methodologies, and perspectives across various disciplines is actively encouraged and supported. This commitment reflects our recognition that many of today's pressing societal issues and research questions demand holistic and multifaceted solutions that transcend the confines of individual academic fields. At PUPQC, we prioritize the breaking down of traditional silos and fostering connections between departments, faculties, and research centers to promote synergistic research, teaching, and problem-solving.
