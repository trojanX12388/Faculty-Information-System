The First Year and Transferees Orientation

The goal of this orientation is to ensure that everyone witnesses the glorious brilliance of the "𝙄𝙨𝙠𝙤𝙡𝙖𝙧𝙨 𝙣𝙜 𝘽𝙖𝙮𝙖𝙣." - Oct 13, 2023

𝙄𝙉 𝙋𝙃𝙊𝙏𝙊𝙎|| "The First Year and Transferees Orientation," commenced at the Winnie Castelo Sports Complex yesterday, 12th of October. Following Mrs. Demelyn E. Monzon's speech addressing the faculty members, Director Jaime P. Gutierrez Jr. alongside Asst. Prof. Philip Soberano also introduced the PUPQC Officials and its Administrative Staffs. As the program director of each course introduced themselves, the first year students discovered a lot more with the degree they had chosen. Aiming to aid the students with regard their queries, the different offices such as the Student and Affairs Services, Registrar Services, Scholarship Services, Guidance and Counseling Services were introduced. Brightening up the said programme, the Mag-aaral ng Unibersidad sa Sining at Adhika (MUSA) performed a highly spirited dance routine. On top of that, all academic and non-academic student organizations presented a brief overview of what their organization is all about. The goal of this orientation is to ensure that everyone witnesses the glorious brilliance of the "𝙄𝙨𝙠𝙤𝙡𝙖𝙧𝙨 𝙣𝙜 𝘽𝙖𝙮𝙖𝙣."

𝗜𝗖𝗬𝗠𝗜 || The Polytechnic University of the Philippines Quezon City branch has started its renovation for the rehabilitation of multi-purpose buildings inside the campus. This comes after the PUP-QC officials, headed by the branch director, Prof. Jaime P. Gutierrez Jr., paid a courtesy visit to the district office of Congressman Ralph Wendel P. Tulfo on May 18, 2023, wherein they came up with a project proposal for said campus considering that PUP-QC is located in District 2.

The project proposal titled “𝗥𝗲𝗵𝗮𝗯𝗶𝗹𝗶𝘁𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗮𝗻𝗱 𝗥𝗲𝗻𝗼𝘃𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗼𝗳 𝗠𝘂𝗹𝘁𝗶-𝗣𝘂𝗿𝗽𝗼𝘀𝗲 𝗕𝘂𝗶𝗹𝗱𝗶𝗻𝗴𝘀 𝗮𝘁 𝗣𝗨𝗣 𝗤𝗖” is expected to be completed with a 10-month completion period for the rehabilitation of the PUP-QC gym and renovation of its offices.

The PUP-QC extends its sincere gratitude to Congressman Ralph Tulfo for the new, progressive, and no-corruption infrastructure development project inside the campus. - Nov 16, 2023
