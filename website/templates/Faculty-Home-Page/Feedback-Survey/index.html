FEEDBACKS

Faculty Feedback and Development Review (FFDR)

Welcome to the Faculty Feedback and Development Review (FFDR) process. This process is designed to provide faculty members with constructive feedback on their performance and to support their ongoing professional development. Through this collaborative effort, we aim to recognize achievements, address challenges, and create a roadmap for growth that aligns with both individual and institutional goals.

Faculty Empowerment and Reflection (FER)

Welcome to the Faculty Empowerment and Reflection (FER) program, a platform for fostering professional growth and creating a supportive environment for our esteemed faculty members. The FER program is dedicated to providing valuable feedback, recognizing accomplishments, and encouraging continuous development.

Academic Excellence Review and Development (AERD)

Welcome to the Academic Excellence Review and Development (AERD) process, a comprehensive initiative designed to support our faculty in achieving their highest potential. Through self-assessment, constructive feedback, and growth planning, the AERD process aims to facilitate continuous improvement, recognize achievements, and provide a roadmap for professional development.
