SELF ASSESSMENT TEST

Taking self-assessment tests plays a crucial role in your professional growth and development within an academic institution. Self-assessment tests provide a structured framework to assess professional journey, set goals, and refine their practices..
Faculty Self-Assessment and Growth Enhancement Tool (F-SAGE)

Welcome to the Faculty Self-Assessment and Growth Enhancement (F-SAGE) tool! This self-assessment is designed to help faculty members reflect on their professional journey, identify strengths, recognize areas for growth, and set goals for their continued development. Your participation in F-SAGE will contribute to fostering a culture of continuous improvement within our academic community.

Faculty Excellence and Growth Assessment (FEGA)

Welcome to the Faculty Excellence and Growth Assessment (FEGA) tool! This assessment is designed to help you explore your strengths as a faculty member and identify areas where further development could lead to even greater impact. By participating in FEGA, you are taking an active step towards enhancing your teaching, research, and overall contribution to our academic community.

TRAINING & WORKSHOPS

FACULTY DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM

The "Enhancing Teaching Excellence" Faculty Development Program is designed to empower faculty members with the knowledge, skills, and resources to excel in their teaching roles. This program focuses on pedagogical strategies, technology integration, and professional growth to ensure faculty are well-prepared to meet the evolving needs of students and higher education.

ADVANCING TEACHING EXCELLENCE

The Advancing Teaching Excellence program is designed to support faculty members in their professional growth and enhance their teaching and leadership skills. This program offers a series of workshops, seminars, and resources aimed at empowering faculty to excel in their roles as educators, mentors, and scholars.
