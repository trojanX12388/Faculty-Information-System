Introduction to Computer Science (CS101)
Instructor Information:

Contact: 0985245877/ jacobjacobyOvalle@gmail.com

Course Description:

This course serves as an introduction to the fundamental concepts of computer science. Topics include programming fundamentals, algorithms, data structures, and problem-solving techniques. The course is designed to provide students with a solid foundation for further studies in computer science.

Learning Objectives

By the end of the course, students should be able to:

  1. Understand the basic principles of programming.
  2. Apply algorithmic thinking to solve computational problems.
  3. Implement and analyze fundamental data structures.
  4. Develop and debug simple programs.
